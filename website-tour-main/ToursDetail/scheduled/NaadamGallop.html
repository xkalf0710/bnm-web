About us

Experience the complete Naadam Festival and embark on an extraordinary 11-day adventure through Mongolia's heartland. Beyond the festival's grandeur, the tour ventures into central Mongolia, offering a unique blend of cultural immersion and thrilling horseback riding. With four full days in the saddle, journey from Ulaan Tsutgalan to the pristine Naiman Lake. Camping under the stars with our trusty steeds, the tour encompasses iconic destinations like Khustai Nuruu National Park, Terelj, the Genghis Khan Statue Complex, and the vibrant city, providing an authentic taste of Mongolian traditions. Don't miss the exhilarating horse riding competition at Khui Doloon Khudag and the serene beauty of Naiman Lake.

Overview
Day 1: Khustai Nuruu National Park

Begin your adventure exploring the wild landscapes of Khustai Nuruu National Park, home to the reintroduced Przewalski's horses. Witness the beauty of Mongolia's diverse flora and fauna while immersing yourself in the unique ecosystem of this protected area.

Day 2: Terelj and Genghis Khan Statue Complex

After a hearty breakfast at Khustai, travel to Terelj and marvel at the iconic Genghis Khan Statue Complex. Discover the history and legacy of Mongolia's legendary leader while surrounded by the breathtaking landscapes of Terelj National Park.

Day 3: Naadam Opening Ceremony in the City

Head to the city to partake in the grand opening ceremony of Naadam. Immerse yourself in the festive atmosphere as you witness traditional sports, cultural performances, and the vibrant spirit of Mongolian celebration.

Day 4: Khui Doloon Khudag and Elsen Tasarkhai

Experience the excitement of the largest horse riding competition in Mongolia at Khui Doloon Khudag. Later, journey to the mesmerizing sand dunes of Elsen Tasarkhai for a delightful dinner and a night spent under the starlit Mongolian sky.

Day 5: Camel Riding at Elsen Tasarkhai, Arvaikheer, and Naiman Lake

Embark on a morning camel ride through the enchanting Elsen Tasarkhai. Enjoy lunch in Arvaikheer, the capital of Uvurkhangai province, before reaching the tranquil shores of Naiman Lake, where the night will be spent in serenity.

Day 6: Horse Riding and Exploration at Naiman Lake

Engage in a day of horse riding and exploration around Naiman Lake, uncovering hidden gems and capturing breathtaking panoramic views from popular vantage points.

Days 7-8: Horse Riding Journey to Ulaan Tsutgalan

Embark on an epic two-day horse riding journey to Ulaan Tsutgalan, traversing diverse landscapes and immersing yourself in the nomadic way of life.

Day 9: Ulaan Tsutgalan Waterfall Exploration

After a rejuvenating lunch, explore the majestic Ulaan Tsutgalan Waterfall, marvel at its natural beauty, and savor the tranquility of the surroundings. Return for dinner and spend the night in this captivating location.

Day 10: Tuvkhun Monastery and Tsenkher Hot Springs

Visit Tuvkhun Monastery atop a mountain, reaching there on horseback. Afterwards, unwind in the therapeutic Tsenkher Hot Springs, enjoying a refreshing evening bath amid picturesque landscapes.

Day 11: Erdenezuu Monastery in Kharkhorin

Conclude your journey with a morning bath at Tsenkher Hot Springs, then head to Kharkhorin. Explore the historic Erdenezuu Monastery, have lunch, and journey back to Ulaanbaatar, cherishing the memories of an unforgettable adventure.
