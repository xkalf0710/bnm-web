About us

Embark on an extraordinary 13-day expedition, the Shamanic Splendors of Mongolia tour, blending cultural immersion, natural wonders, and a unique rendezvous with the mystical realm of shamanism. This remarkable journey takes you from Central Mongolia to the northern reaches, promising encounters with nomadic traditions, the pristine landscapes of Khuvsgul, and an unforgettable participation in the Tenger World Shaman Festival. Delve into the rich tapestry of Mongolia's spiritual heritage and natural beauty as each day unfolds.

Overview
Day 1: Genghis Khan Statue Complex and Terelj Tranquility

Commence your adventure with a visit to the iconic Genghis Khan Statue Complex, followed by a journey to the serene landscapes of Terelj National Park. Explore Turtle Rock and Aryapal Monastery, setting the tone for your mystical journey.

Day 2-3: Tenger World Shaman Festival

Participate in the awe-inspiring Tenger World Shaman Festival, a two-day event featuring over 1000 shamans from Mongolia and around the globe. Engage in rituals, ceremonies, and gain profound insights into shamanism, fostering a deeper connection with the spiritual traditions of the world.

Day 4: Khustai National Park and Elsen Tasarkhai Adventure

After the festival, transition to Khustai National Park, home of the endangered Przewalski's horse. Continue the journey to Elsen Tasarkhai for camel rides amid the mesmerizing dunes, culminating in a tranquil evening.

Day 5: Kharkhorin, Erdenezuu Monastery, and Tsenkher Hot Springs

Visit Kharkhorin, including the historic Erdenezuu Monastery. Proceed to Tsenkher Hot Springs for an evening bath, merging relaxation with cultural exploration.

Day 6: Nomadic Life

Embark on an authentic nomadic experience near Tamir Sum. Ride horses to Taikhar Chuluu and reside amidst the stunning Chuluut Valley landscapes for the night.

Day 7: Khorgo Extinct Volcano and Terkhiin Tsagaan Lake

Drive to the Khorgo Extinct Volcano for a morning hike, followed by a visit to Terkhiin Tsagaan Lake. Spend the night immersed in the tranquility of this picturesque locale.

Day 8: Scenic Drive to Khatgal, Khuvsgul Lake

Embark on a scenic drive to Khatgal, Khuvsgul Lake, enjoying packed food en route. Anticipate the unique beauty of the northern landscapes.

Day 9: Boating at Khuvsgul Lake and Tsaatan Cultural Experience

Delight in a day of boating on Khuvsgul Lake, encountering the Tsaatan people and spending the night immersed in the enchanting surroundings.

Day 10: Drive to Erdenet City via Uran Togoo

Begin the journey back toward the city, with a lunch stop at Uran Togoo, concluding the day in Erdenet City.

Day 11: Amarbaysgalant Monastery Retreat

Drive to the historic Amarbaysgalant Monastery, embracing the spiritual ambiance and spending the night in its serene surroundings.

Day 12: Aglag Buteel Monastery and Ulaanbaatar Return

Visit Aglag Buteel Monastery in the afternoon before returning to Ulaanbaatar in the evening, marking the end of the Numinous Northern Reindeer tour. Revel in the memories and experiences woven into the tapestry of Mongolia's northern landscapes.
