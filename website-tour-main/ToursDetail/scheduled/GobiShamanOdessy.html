About us

Embark on a 15-day expedition, the Singing Southern Dunes tour, a journey into the heart of Southern Mongolia. This immersive experience unfolds with a diverse tapestry of cultural landmarks, breathtaking natural wonders, and the unique thrill of camel riding. The tour's focal points are the enchanting Khongoryn Els sand dunes and the legendary Flaming Cliffs, promising an unforgettable exploration of the Gobi Desert.

Overview
Day 1-2: Genghis Khan Statue Complex and Terelj Tranquility

Begin your adventure at the iconic Genghis Khan Statue Complex, a monumental tribute to Mongolia's historic past. Journey to Terelj, where the serenity of Turtle Rock and Aryapal Monastery welcomes you for a tranquil start.

Day 2: Ulaanbaatar City Exploration

Immerse yourself in the dynamic city of Ulaanbaatar, with visits to the Zaisan Memorial, Sukhbaatar Square, and the museum, offering a blend of cultural richness and historical significance.

Day 3: Baga Gazariin Chuluu Adventure

Explore the rocky landscapes of Baga Gazariin Chuluu, where unique geological formations create a striking backdrop, unveiling the Gobi's natural beauty.

Day 4: Ikh Gazariin Chuluu and Tsagaan Suvraga

Traverse the captivating Ikh Gazariin Chuluu in the afternoon, then journey to Tsagaan Suvraga, known as the "White Stupa," for an evening surrounded by unique formations.

Day 5: Tsagaan Suvraga and Yolyn Am Arrival

Witness the surreal landscapes of Tsagaan Suvraga in the morning, followed by a journey to Yolyn Am. While the night is spent in anticipation, the day promises serene beauty and unique geological formations.

Day 6: Yolyn Am Horse Riding and Khongoryn Els Arrival

Delve into the stunning Yolyn Am, riding horses to explore the area before driving to Khongoryn Els, where evening descends over the expansive sand dunes.

Day 7: Khongoryn Els Dunes Exploration

Experience the magic of Khongoryn Els, riding horses, and reaching the peak of the sand dunes for breathtaking panoramic views, creating lasting memories.

Day 8: Flaming Cliffs and Bayanzag

Venture to the legendary Flaming Cliffs, also known as Bayanzag, where fossil-rich landscapes and vibrant cliffs narrate tales of ancient inhabitants and discoveries.

Day 9: Ongi Monastery and Arvaikheer

Discover the historical ruins of Ongi Monastery in the afternoon, then drive to Arvaikheer, indulging in dinner amidst the tranquility of the Mongolian steppe.

Day 10: Naiman Lake Exploration

Immerse yourself in the pristine beauty of Naiman Lake, exploring the surrounding area and savoring the tranquility of this remote location.

Day 11: Return to Arvaikheer and Elsen Tasarkhai

Journey back to Arvaikheer, indulge in lunch, and drive to Elsen Tasarkhai in the evening for a delightful dinner amidst the diverse landscapes.

Day 12: Khustai National Park and Ulaanbaatar Return

Conclude your adventure with a visit to Khustai National Park, home to the wild Przewalski's horses. As the day unfolds, return to Ulaanbaatar, marking the end of a memorable journey through the Southern wonders of Mongolia.

Day 13-15: Tenger World Shaman Festival

Participate in the awe-inspiring Tenger World Shaman Festival, a two-day event featuring over 1000 shamans from Mongolia and around the globe. Engage in rituals, ceremonies, and gain profound insights into shamanism, fostering a deeper connection with the spiritual traditions of the world.
