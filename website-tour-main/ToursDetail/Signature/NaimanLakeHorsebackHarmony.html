Overview
Day 1: Elsen Tasarkhai & Kharkhorin

Commence your adventure with a drive to Elsen Tasarkhai, where expansive sand dunes beckon for camel rides. Explore the venerable Erdene Zuu Monastery, a testament to Mongolian Buddhism, before culminating the day with a delightful dinner in Kharkhorin.

Day 2: Nomadic Family & Khorkhog Feast

Embark on a morning journey to a nomadic family, crafting your traditional Ger dwelling. Partake in the cherished Mongolian tradition of Khorkhog, indulging in a barbecue feast rich with local flavors.

Day 3: Nomadic Lifestyle & Taikhar Rock

Engage in the nomadic way of life, participating in morning routines like dairy processing and animal care. After lunch, embark on a horse riding odyssey to Taikhar Rock, a majestic natural formation set against the vast Mongolian steppe.

Day 4: Chuluut Valley, Khorgo Volcano & Terkhiin Tsagaan Lake

Embark on a scenic drive to Chuluut Valley, where nature's wonders unfold. Hike to the summit of the dormant Khorgo Volcano, offering panoramic views, and cruise the serene waters of Terkhiin Tsagaan Lake, surrounded by volcanic landscapes.

Day 5: Herding & Traditional Games

Delve deeper into nomadic life by joining herders in their daily tasks. Participate in traditional games, including those featuring bone ankle bones, providing insights into the unique nomadic traditions of Mongolia.

Day 6: Return Journey via Khustai Nuruu Park

Conclude your exploration with a return to the city. Enjoy lunch at Elsen Tasarkhai and explore Khustai Nuruu Park, known for its wild horses and diverse flora, encapsulating the essence of central Mongolia before bidding farewell to this nomadic odyssey.
