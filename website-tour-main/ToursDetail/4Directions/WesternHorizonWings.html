About us

Embark on a 15-day expedition through the captivating landscapes of Western Mongolia on the Western Horizon Wings tour. This journey seamlessly blends cultural encounters, natural wonders, and the unique traditions of the famed eagle hunters. Explore the diverse terrain, from the iconic Genghis Khan Statue Complex to the pristine beauty of Altai Tavan Bogd and the serene Ulaagchnii Khar Lake.

Overview
Day 1-2: Genghis Khan Statue Complex and Terelj Tranquility

Genghis Khan Statue Complex: Begin your adventure at the monumental Genghis Khan Statue Complex, a testament to Mongolia's rich history and a perfect vantage point for panoramic views.
Terelj National Park: Explore Terelj's tranquil landscapes, featuring the symbolic Turtle Rock and the spiritual Aryapal Monastery.

Day 2: Ulaanbaatar City Exploration

Zaisan Memorial: Ascend to Zaisan Memorial for panoramic views of Ulaanbaatar, adorned with impressive sculptures and murals, commemorating Soviet soldiers.
Sukhbaatar Square and Museum: Immerse yourself in the cultural heart of Ulaanbaatar, Sukhbaatar Square, and delve into Mongolia's history at the National Museum.

Day 3: Elsen Tasarkhai and Tsenkher Hot Springs

Elsen Tasarkhai: Journey to the mini-Gobi, Elsen Tasarkhai, boasting sand dunes, rocky formations, and lush greenery.
Tsenkher Hot Springs: Relax in the soothing waters of Tsenkher Hot Springs, surrounded by picturesque hills and valleys.

Day 4: Nomadic Living and Taikhar Chuluu

Nomadic Family Experience: Engage in nomadic living by building a traditional dwelling, experiencing the nomadic way of life, and savoring Mongolian barbecue Khorkhog.
Taikhar Chuluu: Ride horses to the sac ed granite stone, Taikhar Chuluu, amidst the scenic Mongolian steppe.

Day 5: Chuluut Valley, Khorgo Volcano, and Terkhiin Tsagaan Lake

Chuluut Valley: Explore the lush landscapes of Chuluut Valley, traversed by the meandering Chuluut River.
Khorgo Extinct Volcano: Hike to the summit of Khorgo Volcano, capturing panoramic views of the volcanic terrain.
Terkhiin Tsagaan Lake: Discover the beauty of Terkhiin Tsagaan Lake, offering boating opportunities amid the tranquil surroundings.

Day 6: Ulaagchnii Khar Lake

Ulaagchnii Khar Lake: Journey to the remote and pristine Ulaagchnii Khar Lake, surrounded by rugged mountains, creating a serene and secluded atmosphere.

Day 7: Senjit Khad and Mukhart’s River

Senjit Khad: Marvel at the unique geological formations of Senjit Khad, a canyon adorned with towering cliffs and intriguing rock structures.
Mukhart’s River: xplore the scenic Mukhart’s River, meandering through picturesque landscapes.

Day 8: Ulaagchnii Khar Lake Exploration and Khyargas Lake

Ulaagchnii Khar Lake: Immerse yourself in the beauty of Ulaagchnii Khar Lake, enjoying a tranquil morning exploration.
Khyargas Lake: Arrive at the mesmerizing Khyargas Lake, a remote gem surrounded by rolling hills and pristine waters.

Day 9: Journey to Ulgii City

Ulgii City: Venture to the capital of Bayanulgii Province, Ulgii City, experiencing the unique culture and hospitality of Western Mongolia.

Day 10-11: Altai Tavan Bogd and Baga Turgens Waterfall

Altai Tavan Bogd: Stand in awe of the majestic Altai Tavan Bogd, the highest mountain range in Mongolia, boasting breathtaking alpine scenery.
Baga Turgens Waterfall: Embark on a scenic hike to Baga Turgens Waterfall, nestled amid lush vegetation and captivating landscapes.

Day 12: Eagle Hunters Nomadic Family

Eagle Hunters Nomadic Family: Gain insight into the ancient art of eagle hunting, experiencing the unique lifestyle and traditions of Mongolia's revered eagle hunters.

Day 13-14: Return Journey via Govi Altai and Bayankhongor

Govi Alta and Bayankhongor: Traverse through diverse landscapes, from expansive steppes to rocky mountains, offering glimpses of Mongolia's natural diversity.

Day 15: Camel Ride at Elsen Tasarkhai and Khustai National Park

Camel Ride at Elsen Tasarkhai: Enjoy a delightful camel ride in the picturesque Elsen Tasarkhai, surrounded by sand dunes and lush greenery.
Khustai National Park: Explore the unique ecosystem of Khustai National Park, home to the reintroduced Przewalski's horses, concluding your journey through Western Mongolia.
