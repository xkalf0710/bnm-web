The Karakorum Tour offers a rich 6-day exploration of central Mongolia, blending cultural immersion, historical landmarks, and serene natural beauty. Highlights include the spiritual Erdenezuu Monastery and the tranquil shores of Ugii Lake.

Tour itinerary
Day 1: Genghis Khan Statue Complex & Terelj National Park

Overview

Start your journey at the towering Genghis Khan Statue Complex, where you can enjoy panoramic views and learn about Mongolia's history. Then, head to Terelj National Park to explore iconic landmarks such as Turtle Rock and the serene Aryapal Monastery.

Travel distance ~ 120km
Driving time      ~ 3 Hours

Activities
   Visit Genghis Khan Statue Complex
 Explore Turtle Rock and Aryapal Monastery

Meals
Breakfast ~ Ulaanbaatar
Lunch       ~ Genghis Khan Statue Complex
Dinner      ~ Terelj National Park

Day 2: Khustai Nuruu National Park

Overview

Spend the day at Khustai Nuruu National Park, where you’ll spot the famous Przewalski's horses and experience the park's rich wildlife and beautiful landscapes.

Travel distance ~ 200km
Driving time      ~ 4 hours

Activities
  Spot Przewalski's horses in their natural habitat

Meals
Breakfast ~ Terelj National Park
Lunch       ~ Khustai Nuruu Park
Dinner      ~ Khustai Nuruu Park

Day 3: Ugii Lake

Overview

Journey to Ugii Lake, a serene destination perfect for birdwatching, boating, or simply relaxing by the water. Capture the tranquility of the lake through photography or take part in optional fishing activities.

Travel distance ~ 300km
Driving time      ~ 5 Hours

Activities
  Enjoy birdwatching and peaceful activities by Ugii Lake

Meals
Breakfast ~ Khustai Nuruu Park
Lunch       ~ Ugii Lake
Dinner      ~ Ugii Lake

Day 4: Erdenezuu Monastery & Kharkhorin

Overview

Visit the ancient Erdenezuu Monastery, a cornerstone of Mongolian Buddhism. Spend the evening exploring Kharkhorin, the former capital of the Mongol Empire, steeped in history and culture.

Travel distance ~ 120km
Driving time      ~ 2.5 Hours

Activities
 Explore Erdenezuu Monastery
  Walk through the historic town of Kharkhorin

Meals
Breakfast ~ Ugii Lake
Lunch       ~ Erdenezuu Monastery
Dinner      ~ Kharkhorin

Day 5: Nomadic Family & Elsen Tasarkhai

Overview

Spend the morning with a nomadic family, learning about their traditions and daily life. Then, head to Elsen Tasarkhai for a camel ride amidst its stunning sand dunes.

Travel distance ~ 80km
Driving time      ~ 2 Hours

Activities
 Engage with a nomadic family
Ride camels across the Elsen Tasarkhai dunes

Meals
Breakfast ~ Kharkhorin
Lunch       ~ Nomadic Family
Dinner      ~ Elsen Tasarkhai

Day 6: Return to Ulaanbaatar

Overview

Conclude your adventure with a scenic drive back to Ulaanbaatar, making optional stops for souvenirs or sightseeing before wrapping up your journey.

Travel distance ~ 280km
Driving time      ~ 5 Hours

Activities
 Optional stops at local markets or attractions

Meals
Breakfast ~ Elsen Tasarkhai
Lunch       ~ En route
Dinner      ~ Ulaanbaatar

Pricing & Inclusions

All our tours include a professional cameraguide service, capturing your journey with high-quality photos and breathtaking drone footage. We operate only one tour at a time, ensuring a fully personalized and exclusive experience tailored to your needs and preferences.

To further enhance this exclusivity, we offer personalized pricing based on your group size and tour customization. Whether you're traveling solo, with a small group, or as part of a larger expedition, our goal is to provide a high-quality, customized adventure crafted just for you.
 • Daily Rate: Starting from $180 to $230 per person, per day (varies by group size and preferences)
 • Total Tour Cost: 6 Days : $1,080-$1,380 per person

Tour Package Inclusions

Airport Pickup and Departure Transfers: We ensure a hassle-free start and end to your journey, with transportation provided to and from the airport.

4x4 Off-road Vehicle: Travel across Mongolia’s diverse terrain in comfort and safety.


Accommodation: Stay in traditional Ger camps in rural areas and comfortable hotels in cities.

Meals: All meals throughout your journey are included, and fresh water will be provided daily to keep you hydrated on your adventure.

All Activity Fees: From camel riding and horseback adventures to boating, all activity fees are included to provide a fully immersive experience.
Professional Camera Guide: Every tour is led by me, your personal guide and photographer. I’ll capture your journey in high-quality photos and videos, delivered daily so you can share your experiences with friends and family in real-time.
Tour Package Exclusions

International Airfare: You’ll need to arrange your own flights to and from Mongolia.

Personal Expenses: This includes souvenirs, snacks, and beverages beyond those provided.

Optional Activities: Any activities outside the listed itinerary may have an additional cost.

Camera Fees in Museums/Monasteries: Some locations may charge additional fees for photography.

Travel Insurance: We highly recommend travel insurance, but it is not included in the price.
Single Supplement: For those who prefer private accommodation, a single supplement fee applies.
