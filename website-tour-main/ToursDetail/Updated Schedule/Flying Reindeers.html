The Flying Reindeers tour is a 5-day whirlwind adventure, emphasizing time efficiency through air travel to reach Mongolia’s northern wilderness. Explore the stunning Khuvsgul Lake, meet the unique Tsaatan nomads, and immerse yourself in the cultural and natural beauty of the Taiga.

Tour itinerary
Day 1: Arrival at Murun & Khuvsgul Lake Exploration

Overview

Begin with a flight from Ulaanbaatar to Murun, followed by a scenic drive to Khuvsgul Lake. Spend the day exploring the pristine lake, with opportunities for hiking, boating, and soaking in the breathtaking views.

Travel distance ~ 120km
Driving time      ~ 3 Hours

Activities
   Explore the shores of Khuvsgul Lake
  Optional boating on the lake

Meals
Breakfast ~ Ulaanbaatar
Lunch       ~ Khuvsgul Lake
Dinner      ~ Khatgal.Overnight in a lakeside ger camp.

Day 2: Journey to Ulaan Uul

Overview

Travel to Ulaan Uul, a remote village on the edge of the Taiga wilderness. This journey sets the stage for your encounter with the Tsaatan culture.

Travel distance ~ 220km
Driving time      ~ 5 hours

Activities
   Visit Ulaan Uul village and meet locals

Meals
Breakfast ~ Khatgal
Lunch       ~ Ulaan Uul
Dinner     ~ Tsagaan Nuur Overnight in a village ger camp.

Day 3: Experience the Nomadic Lifestyle with Tsaatan

Overview

Venture deep into the Taiga wilderness to meet the Tsaatan nomads, learning about their reindeer herding traditions and unique cultural practices. Immerse yourself in their daily lives and participate in activities like herding and crafting.

Travel distance ~ 50km
Driving time      ~ 4 Hours (on horseback)

Activities
   Spend a day with the Tsaatan nomads
  Capture the beauty of the Taiga and reindeer herding

Meals
Breakfast ~ Ulaan Uul
Lunch       ~ Tsaatan Camp
Dinner      ~ Tsaatan Camp Overnight in the Taiga wilderness.

Day 4: Return to Khatgal

Overview

After a heartfelt farewell to the Tsaatan nomads, journey back to Khatgal, reflecting on your immersive experience in the Taiga. Spend the evening relaxing by the tranquil Khuvsgul Lake.

Travel distance ~ 220km
Driving time      ~ 5 Hours

Activities
   Enjoy leisure time at Khuvsgul Lake

Meals
Breakfast ~ Tsaatan Camp
Lunch       ~ En route to Khatgal
Dinner      ~ Khatgal Overnight in a lakeside ger camp.

Day 5: Fly Back to Ulaanbaatar

Overview

Conclude your journey with a flight from Murun back to Ulaanbaatar, offering stunning aerial views of Mongolia’s vast landscapes.

Travel distance ~ 120km
Driving time      ~ 3 Hours

Activities
   Scenic flight back to Ulaanbaatar

Meals
Breakfast ~ Khatgal
Lunch       ~ Murun
Dinner      ~ Ulaanbaatar Stay at a city hotel.

Pricing & Inclusions

All our tours include a professional cameraguide service, capturing your journey with high-quality photos and breathtaking drone footage. We operate only one tour at a time, ensuring a fully personalized and exclusive experience tailored to your needs and preferences.

To further enhance this exclusivity, we offer personalized pricing based on your group size and tour customization. Whether you're traveling solo, with a small group, or as part of a larger expedition, our goal is to provide a high-quality, customized adventure crafted just for you.
 • Daily Rate: Starting from $180 to $230 per person, per day (varies by group size and preferences)
 • Total Tour Cost: 5 Days : $900-$1,150 per person

Tour Package Inclusions

Airport Pickup and Departure Transfers: We ensure a hassle-free start and end to your journey, with transportation provided to and from the airport.

4x4 Off-road Vehicle: Travel across Mongolia’s diverse terrain in comfort and safety.


Accommodation: Stay in traditional Ger camps in rural areas and comfortable hotels in cities.

Meals: All meals throughout your journey are included, and fresh water will be provided daily to keep you hydrated on your adventure.

All Activity Fees: From camel riding and horseback adventures to boating, all activity fees are included to provide a fully immersive experience.
Professional Camera Guide: Every tour is led by me, your personal guide and photographer. I’ll capture your journey in high-quality photos and videos, delivered daily so you can share your experiences with friends and family in real-time.
Tour Package Exclusions

International Airfare: You’ll need to arrange your own flights to and from Mongolia.

Personal Expenses: This includes souvenirs, snacks, and beverages beyond those provided.

Optional Activities: Any activities outside the listed itinerary may have an additional cost.

Camera Fees in Museums/Monasteries: Some locations may charge additional fees for photography.

Travel Insurance: We highly recommend travel insurance, but it is not included in the price.
Single Supplement: For those who prefer private accommodation, a single supplement fee applies.
