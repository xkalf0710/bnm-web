The Soaring with the Eagles tour is a 4-day adventure into the breathtaking landscapes and rich cultural heritage of Mongolia’s westernmost region. From the snow-capped peaks of the Altai Tavan Bogd Mountains to the cascading Baga Turgens Waterfall, and an intimate encounter with the renowned Eagle Hunters, this tour offers a perfect balance of natural beauty and ancient traditions.

Tour itinerary
Day 1: Arrival at Ulgii City and Journey to Altai Tavan Bogd

Overview

Begin your adventure with a flight to Ulgii City. After an early lunch, embark on a scenic drive to the entrance of Altai Tavan Bogd National Park. Set up camp near the park entrance to prepare for the next day’s exploration.

Travel distance ~ 180km
Driving time      ~ 5 Hours

Activities
   Scenic drive to Altai Tavan Bogd’s entrance
  Enjoy views of the surrounding mountains and wilderness

Meals
Breakfast ~ Ulaanbaatar
Lunch       ~ Ulgii City
Dinner      ~ Altai Tavan Bogd Camp near the park entrance.

Day 2: Altai Tavan Bogd Exploration

Overview

Spend the day exploring Altai Tavan Bogd National Park, home to Mongolia’s highest peaks, glaciers, and ancient petroglyphs. After lunch prepared at the camp, hike through the alpine environment, marveling at the stunning views and historical landmarks. Return to the camp for the evening.

Travel distance~30km(local trekking/driving)
Driving time     ~3 Hours 30 Minutes

Activities
   Hike through Altai Tavan Bogd’s towering peaks and glaciers
  View ancient petroglyphs

Meals
Breakfast ~ Altai Tavan Bogd
Lunch     ~ Altai Tavan Bogd (prepared at camp)
Dinner      ~ Altai Tavan Bogd Camp near the park entrance.

Day 3: Baga Turgens Waterfall and Eagle Hunters Encounter

Overview

Travel to Baga Turgens Waterfall, arriving in the afternoon. Hike to the multi-tiered cascade, enjoying the tranquil environment. Afterward, journey back to Ulgii City, stopping at an Eagle Hunter’s home for lunch and a cultural immersion experience. Spend the night at a local guesthouse in Ulgii.

Travel distance ~ 150km
Driving time      ~ 4 Hours

Activities
   Explore Baga Turgens Waterfall
  Visit an Eagle Hunter family and learn about their traditions

Meals
Breakfast ~ Altai Tavan Bogd
Lunch       ~ Eagle Hunter's Camp
Dinner  ~ Ulgii City Stay in a local guesthouse.

Day 4: Return to Ulaanbaatar

Overview

After breakfast, take an early morning flight back to Ulaanbaatar, concluding your Soaring with the Eagles adventure. Reflect on the stunning landscapes and cultural connections you experienced in Western Mongolia.

Travel distance ~ 180km
Driving time      ~ 5 Hours

Activities
    Flight from Ulgii to Ulaanbaatar

Meals
Breakfast ~ Ugii Lake
Lunch       ~ Not included (tour ends)
Dinner      ~ Not included

Pricing & Inclusions

All our tours include a professional cameraguide service, capturing your journey with high-quality photos and breathtaking drone footage. We operate only one tour at a time, ensuring a fully personalized and exclusive experience tailored to your needs and preferences.

To further enhance this exclusivity, we offer personalized pricing based on your group size and tour customization. Whether you're traveling solo, with a small group, or as part of a larger expedition, our goal is to provide a high-quality, customized adventure crafted just for you.
 • Daily Rate: Starting from $180 to $230 per person, per day (varies by group size and preferences)
 • Total Tour Cost: 4 Days : $720-$920 per person

Tour Package Inclusions

Airport Pickup and Departure Transfers: We ensure a hassle-free start and end to your journey, with transportation provided to and from the airport.

4x4 Off-road Vehicle: Travel across Mongolia’s diverse terrain in comfort and safety.


Accommodation: Stay in traditional Ger camps in rural areas and comfortable hotels in cities.

Meals: All meals throughout your journey are included, and fresh water will be provided daily to keep you hydrated on your adventure.

All Activity Fees: From camel riding and horseback adventures to boating, all activity fees are included to provide a fully immersive experience.
Professional Camera Guide: Every tour is led by me, your personal guide and photographer. I’ll capture your journey in high-quality photos and videos, delivered daily so you can share your experiences with friends and family in real-time.
Tour Package Exclusions

International Airfare: You’ll need to arrange your own flights to and from Mongolia.

Personal Expenses: This includes souvenirs, snacks, and beverages beyond those provided.

Optional Activities: Any activities outside the listed itinerary may have an additional cost.

Camera Fees in Museums/Monasteries: Some locations may charge additional fees for photography.

Travel Insurance: We highly recommend travel insurance, but it is not included in the price.
Single Supplement: For those who prefer private accommodation, a single supplement fee applies.
