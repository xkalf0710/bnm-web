The Eastern Frontier Expedition is a 8-day tour exploring Mongolia's eastern borderlands. From iconic historical sites like Genghis Khan Statue and Dadal to vast steppe landscapes, the tour includes cultural landmarks, tranquil natural settings, and the untouched beauty of Khalkh Gol and Buir Lake. This journey offers an in-depth experience of Mongolia’s history, culture, and nature.

Tour itinerary
Day 1: Ulaanbaatar to Baldan Bereeven Monastery via Genghis Khan Statue

Overview

Start your journey by visiting the Genghis Khan Statue, an iconic monument symbolizing Mongolia's heritage. Continue eastward to Baldan Bereeven Monastery, nestled in the Khentii Mountains.

Travel distance ~ 340km
Driving time      ~ 7 Hours

Activities
  Visit the Genghis Khan Statue.
 Explore Baldan Bereeven Monastery.

Meals
Breakfast ~ Ulaanbaatar
Lunch       ~ En route
Dinner      ~ Baldan Bereeven Hotel

Day 2: Mongolian Queens' Palace, Binder Sum & Yarguitiin Havtsal

Overview

Visit the Mongolian Queens’ Palace, a symbol of Mongolia's royal history, before heading to Binder Sum to explore Yarguitiin Havtsal, Narsan Tugul, and scenic landscapes.

Travel distance ~ 180km
Driving time      ~ 5 Hours

Activities
 Explore Mongolian Queens' Palace.
 Visit Yarguitiin Havtsal and Narsan Tugul

Meals
Breakfast ~ Baldan Bereeven Hotel
Lunch       ~ Binder Sum
Dinner      ~ Binder Sum Ger Camp

Day 3: Binder Sum to Dadal (Birthplace of Genghis Khan)

Overview

Travel to Dadal, the birthplace of Genghis Khan. Visit the Onon River and sacred sites in this legendary location.

Travel distance ~ 200km
Driving time      ~ 5 Hours

Activities
 Visit Dadal, the birthplace of Genghis Khan.
  Enjoy the serene Onon River.

Meals
Breakfast ~ Binder Sum Ger Camp
Lunch       ~ En route
Dinner      ~ Dadal Ger Camp

Day 4: Dadal to Choibalsan

Overview

Depart from Dadal and drive to Choibalsan, the largest city in eastern Mongolia. Explore local landmarks and markets upon arrival.

Travel distance ~ 350km
Driving time      ~ 7 Hours

Activities
Explore Choibalsan’s markets and landmarks.

Meals
Breakfast ~ Dadal Ger Camp
Lunch       ~ En route
Dinner      ~ Choibalsan Hotel

Day 5: Menen Steppe and Khalkh Gol

Overview

Drive through the vast Menen Steppe to Khalkh Gol, a region steeped in history from World War II. Visit the Ikh Burkhant Complex and Khalkh Gol Monuments.

Travel distance ~ 220km
Driving time      ~ 6 Hours

Activities
 Traverse the untouched Menen Steppe.
 Explore the Khalkh Gol Monuments and Ikh Burkhant Complex.

Meals
Breakfast ~ Choibalsan Hotel
Lunch       ~ En route
Dinner      ~ Khalkh Gol Ger Camp

Day 6: Khalkh Gol to Buir Lake

Overview

Journey to Buir Lake, a serene freshwater lake bordering China. Enjoy a relaxing afternoon by the lake’s tranquil waters.

Travel distance ~ 120km
Driving time      ~ 3 Hours

Activities
  Relax by the pristine Buir Lake.

Meals
Breakfast ~ Khalkh Gol Ger Camp
Lunch       ~ En route
Dinner      ~ Buir Lake Ger Camp

Day 7: Buir Lake Exploration and Return to Choibalsan

Overview

Spend the morning exploring the surroundings of Buir Lake before returning to Choibalsan in the evening.

Travel distance ~ 220km
Driving time      ~ 6 Hours

Activities
  Enjoy the beauty of Buir Lake and its wildlife.

Meals
Breakfast ~ Buir Lake Ger Camp
Lunch       ~ En route
Dinner      ~ Choibalsan Hotel

Day 8: Choibalsan to Ulaanbaatar (via Asphalt Road)

Overview

Conclude the tour with a scenic drive back to Ulaanbaatar via the eastern asphalt road.

Travel distance ~ 650km
Driving time      ~ 10-12 Hours

Activities
 Enjoy the landscapes on your return journey.

Meals
Breakfast ~ Choibalsan Hotel
Lunch       ~ En route
Dinner      ~ Ulaanbaatar [Tour ends]

Pricing & Inclusions

All our tours include a professional cameraguide service, capturing your journey with high-quality photos and breathtaking drone footage. We operate only one tour at a time, ensuring a fully personalized and exclusive experience tailored to your needs and preferences.

To further enhance this exclusivity, we offer personalized pricing based on your group size and tour customization. Whether you're traveling solo, with a small group, or as part of a larger expedition, our goal is to provide a high-quality, customized adventure crafted just for you.
 • Daily Rate: Starting from $180 to $230 per person, per day (varies by group size and preferences)
 • Total Tour Cost: 8 Days : $1,440-$1,840 per person

Tour Package Inclusions

Airport Pickup and Departure Transfers: We ensure a hassle-free start and end to your journey, with transportation provided to and from the airport.

4x4 Off-road Vehicle: Travel across Mongolia’s diverse terrain in comfort and safety.


Accommodation: Stay in traditional Ger camps in rural areas and comfortable hotels in cities.

Meals: All meals throughout your journey are included, and fresh water will be provided daily to keep you hydrated on your adventure.

All Activity Fees: From camel riding and horseback adventures to boating, all activity fees are included to provide a fully immersive experience.
Professional Camera Guide: Every tour is led by me, your personal guide and photographer. I’ll capture your journey in high-quality photos and videos, delivered daily so you can share your experiences with friends and family in real-time.
Tour Package Exclusions

International Airfare: You’ll need to arrange your own flights to and from Mongolia.

Personal Expenses: This includes souvenirs, snacks, and beverages beyond those provided.

Optional Activities: Any activities outside the listed itinerary may have an additional cost.

Camera Fees in Museums/Monasteries: Some locations may charge additional fees for photography.

Travel Insurance: We highly recommend travel insurance, but it is not included in the price.
Single Supplement: For those who prefer private accommodation, a single supplement fee applies.
